Cookie policy

How this site uses cookies and similar storage

This is a draft policy for the in-progress website. It is designed around UK cookie requirements, with optional analytics disabled until a visitor actively opts in.

Essential storage

What is always used

This site uses a small item of browser storage to remember your cookie choice. That preference storage is used so the site does not ask you for the same choice on every page view.

No Google Analytics or other optional analytics tracking should run until you choose to allow it.

Optional analytics

What happens if analytics are enabled later

If Google Analytics is added to the live site, it should only load after a visitor has actively accepted analytics cookies. If analytics are rejected, the analytics tag should remain blocked.

The banner on this site is currently built around that safer “no analytics before consent” approach.

Your choices

How visitors can manage preferences

  • Choose to accept analytics cookies.
  • Choose to reject analytics cookies.
  • Reopen the cookie banner later using the Cookie settings button.

Before launch

This page should be reviewed alongside the site privacy policy and the final Google Analytics setup. If a legal or compliance adviser is involved, the wording should be checked before the site goes live.
